About the Project

Legacy Works is a complete branding package for an antique furniture chain. The goal was to merge traditional elegance with modern design. Deliverables include logos, business cards, letterhead, banner, and social media mockups.

Design Process

  • Research: Competitor & antique brand analysis.
  • Concept Ideation: Logo, typography, color palette concepts.
  • Wireframing: Layouts for cards, letterhead, social media posts.
  • Final Design: Completed logo, banner, business cards, letterhead, and social media mockups.
