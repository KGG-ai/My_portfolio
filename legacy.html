About the Project

Legacy Works was designed as a complete branding package to represent an antique furniture chain. The goal was to merge the elegance of traditional design with the sleekness of modern branding. Deliverables include logos, business cards, letterhead, banner, and social media kit.

Process

The design process followed a structured approach to ensure timeless branding that resonates with Legacy Works’ audience:

  • Research & Discovery: Studied the brand history, competitors, and target audience.
  • Concept Development: Created mood boards, sketches, and initial logo ideas.
  • Design Refinement: Finalized logo, color palette, typography, and visual elements.
  • Collateral Design: Designed business cards, letterhead, banners, and social media mockups.
  • Review & Feedback: Iterative feedback with stakeholders to refine and finalize all assets.
  • Delivery: Provided a complete branding package ready for implementation across all platforms.
